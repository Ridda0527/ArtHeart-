What is Art from the Heart? 💕

We're a student-led club from Little Rock Central High School dedicated to bringing art, wellness, and joy to kids across our community — completely free!

Every week we assemble creative goody bags and drop them off at the Central Arkansas Library System. Each kit comes with a fun project that teaches kids about health, the human body, and mental well-being. 🌟
