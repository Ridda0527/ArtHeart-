Mission Statement 🌸

In our own words~

Art from the Heart is dedicated to enriching the well-being of our community through creativity and compassion. Our goal is to ignite a love for art and empower kids to express their imagination. At the same time, we emphasize the importance of building healthy habits and prioritizing mental well-being. Through hands-on learning, our program introduces children and teens to the wonders of the human body, combining art and education in a fun and impactful way.

We assemble weekly goody bags to drop off at libraries that are part of the Central Arkansas Library System. These goody bags, along with lessons and tutorials on this website, teach kids of all ages about health and wellness!

Not everyone has access to art lessons or strong role models for healthy living. That's why Art from the Heart is here to give kids the tools to discover their artistic potential, explore their creativity, and learn about taking care of their bodies and minds. Through these goody bags, we hope to leave a lasting impression that inspires growth, confidence, and wellness in every child we reach. 💕
